How To Play:

The aim of this game is to remember where all the green tiles are. The tiles will be hidden, you need to click on the squares that you think are hiding a green tile.

1: Click Show tiles.

2: Memorize where all the green dots are.

3: Click play game.

4: Click the blank squares where the green tiles are.

5: Three wrong answers and the game is over.

6: Click restart to play again.
