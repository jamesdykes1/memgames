How To Play:

The aim of this game is to memorise where the green tiles are. Once hidden, select where the tiles were before the timer ends!

1: Click Show tiles.

2: Memorize where all the green dots are.

3: Click play game.

4: Click the blank squares where the green tiles are.

5: Three wrong answers and the game is over.

6: Click restart to play again.
