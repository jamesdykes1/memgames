How To Play:

The aim of this game is to memorise the shape shown 2 previous to find a match.

1. Click the play button.

2. Memorise the shapes that flash on the screen.

3. If the current shape matches the shape shown 2 previous click the match button.

4. The game is won when you score 5 points.
