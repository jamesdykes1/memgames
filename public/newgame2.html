How To Play:

The aim of this game is to match the text in the blue box with the ink colour in the red box.

1. Click the play button

2. If the text and colour match, click the match button.

3. If the text and colour DO NOT match, click the different button.

4. You have 20 seconds to score as many points as possible.

5. To play again click restart and play when you are ready.
